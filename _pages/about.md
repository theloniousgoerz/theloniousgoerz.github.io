I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in the [Department of Sociology](https://sociology.cornell.edu/) and the [Brooks School of Public Policy](https://publicpolicy.cornell.edu/) at Cornell University. 
I apply computational and causal inference tools to study these topics using historical and contemporary administrative and survey data.  Prior to graduate study, I was a pre-doctoral research fellow in sociology and economics at Johns Hopkins University, and I graduated from the University of Washington, Seattle.
